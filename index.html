Chairman's Remarks

Dear brothers and sisters in Islam,

It is with great pleasure and gratitude to Allah that I address you today as we continue our journey of faith and service at Nasrin Masjid. Our mosque has grown tremendously over the past year, thanks to your unwavering support and dedication.

We have achieved significant milestones, from improving our physical infrastructure to strengthening our community bonds. The installation of security cameras, construction of a new kitchen, and organization of various educational and social events are testaments to what we can accomplish when we work together with sincerity and unity.

As we look to the future, I encourage all students to actively participate in our programs and initiatives. Your involvement is crucial to maintaining the vibrant Islamic environment we've cultivated at Bishop Stuart University. Let us continue to support one another in righteousness and piety, and may Allah accept all our efforts.

Usaama Ismael
Chairman, BSUMSA
